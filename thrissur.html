Kozhikode, Kerala

A city along the Malabar Coast in the state of Kerala in India. Known as the City of Spices[1], Kozhikode is listed among the UNESCO's Cities of Literature.t is the largest city in the region known as the Malabar Coast and was the capital of the British-era Malabar district. It was the capital of an independent kingdom ruled by the Samoothiris.
